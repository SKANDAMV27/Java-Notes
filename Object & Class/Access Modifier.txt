Access Modifier

It has Three types to Declare the method

	1.public
	2.Protected
	3.Private

1.public access Modifier it can access to all classes everywhere.

2.If the class has no modifier then its visible only within its own package

3.private modifier it can access only its own class.

4.Protecyed Modifier it can accessed in the own package and in additional by subclass of its another package.

Access level modifiers determine whether other classes can use a particular field or invoke a particular method. There are two levels of access control:

At the top level—public, or package-private (no explicit modifier).
At the member level—public, private, protected, or package-private (no explicit modifier).
